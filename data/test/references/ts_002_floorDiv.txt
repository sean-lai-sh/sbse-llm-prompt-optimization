The `floorDiv` function performs integer floor division on two numbers, returning the largest integer less than or equal to the quotient `a / b`. It throws an `Error` with the message "division by zero" if `b` is `0`. Unlike truncation-based division, it uses `Math.floor`, so results round toward negative infinity (e.g., `floorDiv(-7, 2)` returns `-4` rather than `-3`).
