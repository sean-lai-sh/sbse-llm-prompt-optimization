Validates a card number string using the Luhn checksum algorithm, stripping spaces and ignoring any non-digit characters before processing. It doubles every second digit from the right, subtracting 9 if the doubled value exceeds 9, sums all digits, and returns True only when the total is divisible by 10. Returns False for inputs with fewer than two digits; runs in O(n) time and O(n) space due to the digit list.
