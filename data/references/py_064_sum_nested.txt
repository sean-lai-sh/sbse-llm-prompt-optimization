Recursively sums all numeric (int or float) leaves contained in an arbitrarily nested list/tuple structure, returning the total as an int or float. Non-numeric, non-iterable values contribute 0 to the sum, so strings, None, and other types are silently ignored rather than raising. Runs in O(n) time relative to total leaf count with recursion depth equal to the structure's maximum nesting depth; bool values are counted as ints because of isinstance semantics.
