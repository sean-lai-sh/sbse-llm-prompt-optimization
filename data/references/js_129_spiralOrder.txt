Traverses a 2D matrix in clockwise spiral order starting from the top-left, returning the visited values as a flat array. It maintains four shrinking boundaries (top, bottom, left, right) and in each loop iteration walks the top row left-to-right, the right column top-to-bottom, conditionally the bottom row right-to-left, and conditionally the left column bottom-to-top, then contracts the relevant boundary. The two conditional passes are guarded by top<=bottom and left<=right to avoid double-visiting rows or columns in non-square or single-row/column residuals. Empty or non-array input returns []; complexity is O(rows * cols) time and O(1) extra space beyond the output.
