Solves the classic minimum-coin-change problem with bottom-up DP, computing dp[i] as the fewest coins from denominations summing to amount i and a parent[i] array recording which coin was used to reach that amount. Time complexity is O(amount * denominations.length) and space is O(amount), and after filling the table the function reconstructs the actual coin sequence by walking parent backwards from amount to 0. Returns a CoinChangeResult; impossible cases (including negative amount) yield {minCoins: -1, coins: []}, while amount of 0 yields {minCoins: 0, coins: []}, and the sentinel INF = amount + 1 marks unreachable subproblems.
