Computes the total volume of water trapped between bars in a histogram given by the height list, using two precomputed arrays left_max[i] and right_max[i] holding the running maxima from each side. For each column the trapped water is max(0, min(left_max[i], right_max[i]) - height[i]), summed into the total; runs in O(n) time and O(n) extra space. Returns (0, []) for empty input and (0, [0]*n) for n < 3, raises ValueError on any negative height, and only populates the per-column water list when return_per_column is True.
