Builds a prefix trie from the given list of words and processes a sequence of ("search", w), ("prefix", p), or ("delete", w) queries, returning a list of booleans for each query in order. Insert, search, and starts_with each walk the trie character-by-character in O(len(word)) time using a children dict and an is_end flag, while delete unmarks the terminal node and prunes any now-empty ancestor branches via a recorded path. The function returns False for delete attempts on words not present and silently ignores queries whose op tag is none of the three supported values.
