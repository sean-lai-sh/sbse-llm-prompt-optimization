Builds a 1-indexed Fenwick (binary indexed) tree over an integer array and answers a sequence of "sum", "prefix", and "update" queries, returning the results of the read queries in order. Internal helpers update and prefix_sum each take O(log n) time using the standard i & -i bit trick, and update queries adjust the tree by the delta between the new and old values while keeping nums in sync. Range sums are computed as prefix_sum(hi) - prefix_sum(lo-1) (with a special case when lo == 0), and queries with unrecognized op tags are silently skipped.
