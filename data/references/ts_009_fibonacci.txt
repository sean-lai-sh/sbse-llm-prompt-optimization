Computes the nth Fibonacci number iteratively using two rolling variables a and b updated via destructuring assignment. It accepts a non-negative number and returns a number in O(n) time and O(1) space, throwing a RangeError for negative inputs and short-circuiting for n of 0 or 1. Results lose precision once they exceed Number.MAX_SAFE_INTEGER around n = 79 because of double-precision arithmetic.
