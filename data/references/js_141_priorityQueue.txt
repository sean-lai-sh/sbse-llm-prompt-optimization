Runs a binary-heap priority queue (min-heap by default) against a sequence of operations and returns one result entry per op. It supports push (sift-up), pop (swap root with last, pop, sift-down), peek, size, and a bulk build that heapifies an existing array in O(n) by sifting down from the last internal node. A custom comparator may be supplied; the default (a, b) => a - b yields min-heap ordering, and any comparator returning negative for "smaller" is honored. push/pop run in O(log n); pop returns undefined on an empty heap rather than throwing.
