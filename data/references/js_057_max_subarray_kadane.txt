Implements Kadane's algorithm to find the contiguous subarray of nums with the maximum sum in O(n) time and O(1) auxiliary space (plus the returned slice). It tracks a running currentSum, resetting to nums[i] whenever extending the previous subarray would yield a smaller sum than starting fresh, and updates maxSum along with the maxStart and maxEnd indices whenever a new best is observed. It returns an object containing maxSum, the slice subarray between the two indices, and the boundary indices themselves. The function throws an Error when nums is null/undefined or empty, and correctly handles all-negative inputs by returning the single largest element.
