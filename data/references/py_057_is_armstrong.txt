Determines whether an integer n is an Armstrong (narcissistic) number, meaning the sum of each of its digits raised to the power of the digit count equals the number itself. It uses abs(n) so negative inputs are checked against their magnitude, computes the digit count via str length, and compares the power-sum to abs(n), returning a bool. Runs in O(d) time where d is the number of digits and treats 0 and single-digit numbers as Armstrong numbers.
