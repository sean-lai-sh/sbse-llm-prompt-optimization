Computes the product of all numbers in the array nums by reducing with multiplication and an initial accumulator of 1, returning a number in O(n) time and O(1) extra space. Because the seed is 1, an empty input correctly yields the multiplicative identity. Any NaN element propagates to a NaN result, a single 0 forces the entire product to 0, and very large products overflow to +/-Infinity under IEEE-754.
