Performs exponential search on a sorted list of integers for a target, returning its index or None. It first doubles an index i from 1 until arr[i] exceeds the target or the array end is reached, then runs a standard binary search over the bounded range [i//2, min(i, n-1)]. Overall time complexity is O(log n) with O(1) space, empty lists and a target equal to arr[0] are handled by explicit early returns, and the input must be sorted in ascending order.
