Interleaves two strings s1 and s2 character by character, then appends any remaining tail from the longer string after the shared-length prefix has been processed. Uses two indices advanced in lockstep through a while loop and a list accumulator joined at the end for O(n + m) time and space, where n and m are the input lengths. Handles strings of unequal length by appending the leftover slice of whichever string is longer, and returns one input verbatim when the other is empty.
