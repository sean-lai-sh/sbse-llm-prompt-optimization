Returns True if the input sentence is a pangram (contains every letter of the English alphabet at least once) and False otherwise. It checks whether the set of lowercase ASCII letters 'abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz' is a subset of the set of characters in the lowercased sentence. Time and space are O(n), the check is case-insensitive, ignores non-letter characters by virtue of the subset comparison, and treats only the 26 ASCII letters (so accented characters do not count toward coverage).
