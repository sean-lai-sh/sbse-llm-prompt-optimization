Generates the Collatz sequence starting from a positive integer n by iteratively halving even values and applying 3n+1 to odd values until reaching 1. It takes an int n and returns the full list of intermediate values including the initial n and the terminating 1. Raises ValueError when n is not a positive integer; runs in unbounded time since the Collatz conjecture is unproven, though it is iterative rather than recursive and uses O(k) space where k is the sequence length.
