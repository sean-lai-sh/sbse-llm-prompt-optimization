Performs pattern matching using the Z-algorithm by concatenating pattern + "$" + text and computing the Z-array (length of the longest substring starting at each position that matches a prefix of the concatenation) in linear time via the [l, r] window optimization. After building, it scans positions past the separator and records a match in the original text whenever the Z-value equals the pattern length, returning both the trimmed Z-array (offset by pattern.length + 1) and the match indices. Runs in O(n + m) time and O(n + m) space; assumes the "$" sentinel does not appear inside text or pattern.
