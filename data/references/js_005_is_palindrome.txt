Determines whether the input string str is a palindrome by lowercasing it, stripping all non-alphanumeric ASCII characters via a regex, and comparing the cleaned string to its reverse. Returns a boolean and runs in O(n) time and O(n) extra space. Treats whitespace, punctuation, and case as insignificant, but only considers a-z and 0-9 as valid characters and therefore mishandles accented or non-ASCII letters.
