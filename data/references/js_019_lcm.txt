Computes the least common multiple of integers a and b by first calculating their greatest common divisor inline using the Euclidean algorithm and then returning the absolute value of a*b divided by that gcd. Runs in O(log min(a, b)) time and O(1) space. Returns NaN when both a and b are 0 due to division by zero, and may overflow JavaScript's safe integer range for large operands because a*b is computed before the division.
