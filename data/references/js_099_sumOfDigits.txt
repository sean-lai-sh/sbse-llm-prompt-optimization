Returns the sum of the decimal digits of an integer n by taking Math.abs(n), converting to a string, splitting into individual characters, and reducing them to a sum after coercing each via Number. Runs in O(d) time and O(d) space where d is the digit count. The use of Math.abs makes negative inputs behave like their positive counterparts. For non-integer floats the decimal point character coerces to NaN under Number, which propagates a NaN result; very large integers beyond Number.MAX_SAFE_INTEGER may also stringify in scientific notation, again producing NaN-contaminated digits.
