Determines whether the round, square, and curly brackets in string s are properly matched and nested by performing a single left-to-right scan with an explicit stack. Opening brackets are pushed onto the stack and each closing bracket must match the top of the stack via a closer-to-opener mapping; a mismatch or empty stack on a closer immediately returns False. The algorithm runs in O(n) time and O(n) auxiliary space, returns True for the empty string, and ignores all non-bracket characters in s.
