Validates an email string by trimming surrounding whitespace and testing it against a simple regex requiring one or more non-whitespace, non-"@" characters, an "@", more such characters, a literal dot, and a final non-empty segment. Returns true on match and false otherwise. The check is intentionally lax compared to RFC 5322: it accepts many invalid addresses (consecutive dots, missing TLD validation) and rejects valid ones containing quoted local parts or comments.
