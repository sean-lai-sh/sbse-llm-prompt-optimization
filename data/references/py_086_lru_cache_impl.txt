A decorator factory that implements a true LRU cache with the given capacity using collections.OrderedDict, moving accessed keys to the end on hits and evicting the least-recently-used entry (popitem(last=False)) when capacity is exceeded. Cache keys are built from positional args and sorted kwargs, falling back to str() representations when the natural key is unhashable. Tracks hits and misses, exposes cache_info(), cache_clear(), and cache_peek() helpers plus the original __wrapped__ reference, and validates capacity >= 1 at decoration time; not thread-safe.
