Solves the 0/1 knapsack problem with parallel weights and values arrays and an integer capacity using a standard (n+1) by (capacity+1) DP table, returning an object with maxValue and the list of selected item indices. Each cell dp[i][w] is filled in O(1) by choosing the better of skipping item i-1 or taking it when its weight fits, yielding O(n*capacity) time and space (pseudo-polynomial). After filling the table it backtracks from dp[n][capacity], comparing each row to the row above to decide whether item i-1 was included and decrementing the remaining capacity accordingly. It short-circuits to {maxValue: 0, items: []} when n is 0 or capacity is 0, and assumes non-negative integer weights.
