Solves the 0/1 knapsack problem using a (n+1) x (capacity+1) DP table where dp[i][c] is the maximum value achievable using the first i items within capacity c, returning a tuple (max_value, selected_indices). Validates that weights and values have equal length and that capacity is non-negative, raising ValueError otherwise. When return_items is True it backtracks through the table to recover the indices of chosen items in ascending order; runs in O(n * capacity) time and space.
