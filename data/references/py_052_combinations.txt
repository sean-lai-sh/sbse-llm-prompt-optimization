Computes the binomial coefficient C(n, r) iteratively using the multiplicative formula, taking integers n and r and returning the integer count of r-element subsets of an n-element set. It returns 0 when r is negative or greater than n and 1 for the boundary cases r==0 or r==n, otherwise it shrinks r to min(r, n-r) for efficiency and accumulates the product in O(r) time and O(1) space. The integer division at each step preserves exact integer results without overflow concerns in Python.
