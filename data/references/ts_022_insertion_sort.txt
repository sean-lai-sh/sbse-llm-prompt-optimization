Sorts a numeric array in ascending order using insertion sort, which scans from left to right and shifts larger elements one position to the right to insert each key into its correct place. It takes a number[] and returns a new sorted number[] in O(n^2) worst-case time, O(n) best-case time on nearly sorted data, and O(n) space due to the leading shallow copy. The algorithm is stable and operates on the copy without mutating the caller's array.
