Runs a disjoint-set (union-find) data structure over n elements 0..n-1 against a list of operations and returns per-operation result objects. It implements find with recursive path compression and union by rank with subtree-size tracking, plus connected, componentSize, and countComponents helpers. The dispatcher handles "union", "find", "connected", "componentSize", and "countComponents", recording the merged flag, root, boolean, size, or count respectively. The amortized cost per find/union is effectively O(alpha(n)); countComponents is O(n) since it scans every element to collect roots.
