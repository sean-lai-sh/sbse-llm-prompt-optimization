Computes the weighted Levenshtein edit distance between strings s1 and s2 using a (m+1) x (n+1) DP table where the boundary row/column are seeded with cumulative insert and delete costs and each interior cell is the min of delete, insert, and replace transitions (or carried over on a character match). Returns a tuple (distance, operations) where operations is None unless return_operations is True, in which case it backtracks from (m, n) to produce a human-readable list of "keep", "delete", "insert", and "replace" steps in forward order. Runs in O(m*n) time and space and supports custom insert_cost, delete_cost, and replace_cost weights.
