Computes the median of the merged order of two sorted numeric arrays in O(log(min(m, n))) time via binary search over partition positions. It first ensures a is the shorter array (recursing with arguments swapped if not), then binary-searches a partition partA in a and the complementary partB in b such that the left halves' max values are less than or equal to the right halves' min values, using sentinel +/-Infinity for out-of-range partitions. When the correct partition is found it returns the average of the two middle values for even total length or the larger left-side value for odd length.
