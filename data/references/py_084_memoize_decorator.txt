A decorator factory that caches function results in an in-memory dict keyed by a tuple constructed from positional args, sorted kwargs, and (optionally) argument types when typed=True. Cache hits and misses are tracked in nonlocal counters, and when max_size is set and exceeded the oldest entry (first by insertion order) is evicted before adding the new one, giving FIFO rather than true LRU semantics. Exposes wrapper.cache, cache_info(), and cache_clear() helpers; arguments must be hashable to participate in the key tuple, and an object() sentinel is inserted between args and kwargs to avoid key collisions.
