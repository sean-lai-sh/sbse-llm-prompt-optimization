Determines whether two strings are anagrams by lowercasing them, removing whitespace, sorting the remaining characters, and comparing the resulting normalized strings. It accepts two strings and returns a boolean, running in O(n log n) time due to the sort and O(n) space for the intermediate arrays. The check is case-insensitive and whitespace-insensitive but treats punctuation as significant, and it splits per UTF-16 code unit so multi-code-unit characters such as emoji may be compared incorrectly.
