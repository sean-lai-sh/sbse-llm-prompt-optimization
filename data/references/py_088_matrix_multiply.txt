Computes the standard matrix product of two 2D lists a (rows_a x cols_a) and b (rows_b x cols_b), returning a new rows_a-by-cols_b matrix; raises ValueError on empty inputs, dimension mismatch (cols_a != rows_b), or jagged rows. The triple loop is ordered i-k-j and skips inner iterations when a[i][k] == 0, providing a small sparsity optimization while still running in O(rows_a * cols_a * cols_b) time. When mod is provided, every entry of the result is reduced modulo mod in a final pass; the input matrices are not mutated.
