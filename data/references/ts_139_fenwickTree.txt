Constructs a 1-indexed Fenwick (Binary Indexed) Tree over the nums array of size n+1 and returns update, prefixSum, and rangeSum closures supporting point updates and prefix/range sum queries. The build initializes the tree by applying buildUpdate(i, nums[i]) for each index, and update/prefixSum use the i += i & -i and i -= i & -i bit tricks to walk to parents and ancestors respectively. Each operation runs in O(log n) and rangeSum(left, right) returns prefixSum(right) - prefixSum(left - 1), with a special case avoiding the negative index when left is 0; update also mutates the underlying nums array.
