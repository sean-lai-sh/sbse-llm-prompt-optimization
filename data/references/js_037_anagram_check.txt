Determines whether str1 and str2 are anagrams by short-circuiting to false when their lengths differ, then comparing case-insensitive sorted character sequences for equality. Returns a boolean and runs in O(n log n) time and O(n) space due to the split, sort, and join in the inline normalize helper. Treats whitespace and punctuation as significant characters and operates on UTF-16 code units, so it does not handle locale-specific casing or grapheme clusters correctly.
