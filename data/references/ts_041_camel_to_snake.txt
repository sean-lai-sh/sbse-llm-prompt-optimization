Converts a camelCase or PascalCase identifier to snake_case using two regular expression passes that insert underscores at the boundaries of acronym-then-word and lowercase-or-digit-then-uppercase, followed by a final toLowerCase. It takes a string and returns a string in O(n) time and O(n) space. The two-stage pattern correctly handles consecutive uppercase runs such as "HTTPServer" by producing "http_server" rather than splitting between every capital letter.
