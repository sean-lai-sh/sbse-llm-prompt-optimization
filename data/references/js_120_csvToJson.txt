Parses a CSV string into an array of row objects keyed by the header row, supporting a configurable delimiter (default comma). The internal parseLine routine walks each line character by character, toggling an inQuotes flag on unescaped double quotes, treating "" inside quotes as a literal quote, and only splitting on the delimiter when outside quotes. The first non-empty trimmed line supplies the headers and each subsequent line is mapped position-by-position, with missing trailing fields defaulting to "". Non-string or empty input, or fewer than two lines, yields []; embedded newlines inside quoted fields are not handled because rows are split on "\n" before quote-aware parsing.
