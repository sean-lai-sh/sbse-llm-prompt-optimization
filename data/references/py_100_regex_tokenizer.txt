Tokenizes a text string by combining a dict of named regex patterns (defaulting to FLOAT, INTEGER, STRING, IDENTIFIER, OPERATOR, PUNCTUATION, WHITESPACE, NEWLINE) into a single alternation with named groups and matching greedily from left to right. Returns a list of (token_kind, lexeme) tuples; whitespace and newline tokens are dropped when skip_whitespace is True, and unmatched positions either raise ValueError (when raise_on_unknown is True) or emit a one-character ('UNKNOWN', char) token before advancing by one. Validates each user-supplied pattern compiles, supports a case_sensitive flag that toggles re.IGNORECASE, and raises TypeError when text is not a string; per-call cost is O(n) in the input length times regex backtracking.
