Performs Fibonacci search on a sorted list of integers, returning the index of the target or None if not present. It first grows three consecutive Fibonacci numbers until the largest exceeds n, then repeatedly probes index min(offset+fib2, n-1), shrinking the active Fibonacci window toward the left or right depending on whether the probed value is greater than or less than the target. Time complexity is O(log n) using only addition and subtraction (no division), space is O(1), and a final check handles the case where the last remaining element at offset+1 matches the target.
