Recursively flattens a nested dict into a single-level dict by joining nested keys with the separator sep (default '.'), prefixing each key with parent_key as the recursion descends. Non-dict values become leaves keyed by the dotted path; dict values are recursively flattened. Runs in O(n) time over total leaves and recursion depth equal to the maximum nesting depth; lists and other container types are treated as opaque leaf values rather than being flattened.
