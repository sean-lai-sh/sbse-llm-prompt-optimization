Computes the maximum rectangular area trappable between two vertical lines whose heights are given in the heights array, using the two-pointer technique. It starts with left and right at the array ends, tracks the running maxWater as width times the lower of the two heights, then advances whichever pointer references the shorter line so a potentially taller boundary may yield a larger area. Runs in O(n) time and O(1) space; with fewer than two elements the loop never executes and 0 is returned.
