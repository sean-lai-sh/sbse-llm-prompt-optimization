Runs the Bellman-Ford single-source shortest-path algorithm over a list of weighted directed edges, returning per-vertex distances, parent pointers, a hasNegativeCycle flag, and a getPath closure for reconstruction. It initializes distances to Infinity (0 at the source) and performs up to V - 1 full edge relaxation passes, breaking out early when a pass changes nothing, then performs one more pass to detect a negative cycle (any further relaxable edge sets the flag). getPath walks parents from a target back to the source, returning [] when the target is unreachable or when a cycle is encountered via a visited set; total time is O(V * E).
