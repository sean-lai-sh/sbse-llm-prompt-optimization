Reverses the order of whitespace-delimited words in a string by trimming leading and trailing whitespace, splitting on runs of whitespace, reversing the resulting array, and rejoining with single spaces. It takes a string and returns a string in O(n) time and O(n) space. Internal multi-whitespace and tab runs are collapsed to a single space in the output, and an empty or whitespace-only input becomes the empty string after trimming.
