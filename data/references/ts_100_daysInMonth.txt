Returns the number of days in the given 1-indexed month of the given year by constructing a Date with day 0 of the next month, which the Date constructor normalizes to the last day of the requested month, and reading getDate(). It implicitly handles leap years through the underlying Date object and runs in O(1). The function expects month in the human 1-12 range (passing 12 correctly yields December's 31 days via January day 0 of year+1); month values outside that range silently roll over via Date's normalization rather than raising an error.
