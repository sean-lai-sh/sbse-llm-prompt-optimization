Performs lightweight syntactic validation of an email address string and returns a boolean. It rejects non-strings, empty/whitespace-only inputs, and addresses longer than 254 characters, then matches a regex requiring a non-space local part, an "@", and a domain with a dot followed by at least two non-space characters. After regex validation it splits on "@" and additionally rejects local parts longer than 64 characters and domains starting or ending with a dot. It is not a full RFC 5322 validator and accepts many syntactically odd but regex-conformant addresses.
