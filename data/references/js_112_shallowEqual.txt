Performs a one-level structural comparison between two values, returning true only when they are strictly equal or are non-null objects with the same own keys and strictly equal corresponding values. It first checks reference identity, then rejects non-objects and nulls, then compares Object.keys lengths before iterating with hasOwnProperty membership and === per key. It does not recurse into nested values, so deeply nested structures with equal contents but distinct references will compare unequal. Arrays and plain objects are treated identically since Object.keys covers both.
