Finds all unique triplets in nums that sum to zero by sorting the list in place and then, for each anchor index i, using a two-pointer scan with lo and hi over the remaining suffix to locate complementary pairs. Duplicate triplets are skipped by advancing past equal anchor values and by skipping equal lo/hi values after a successful match, yielding O(n^2) time and O(1) extra space beyond the sort and result list. The function mutates the caller's list via sort and returns triplets as tuples in non-decreasing order.
