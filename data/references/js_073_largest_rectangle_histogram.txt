Computes the area of the largest rectangle that fits inside a histogram given by the heights array using a monotonic increasing stack of indices in O(n) time and O(n) space. It iterates one position past the end with a sentinel height of 0 to flush the stack, and whenever the current bar is shorter than the top of the stack it pops, computing area = poppedHeight * width where width is i if the stack becomes empty or i - newTop - 1 otherwise. The maximum area encountered is tracked across all pops and returned. It returns 0 for null/undefined or empty input and assumes non-negative bar heights.
