Performs the standard triple-nested-loop multiplication of two numeric matrices a (rowsA x colsA) and b (colsA x colsB), returning a freshly allocated rowsA x colsB result matrix. The result is initialized to zeros via Array.from with Array.fill, then each result[i][j] accumulates the dot product of row i of a with column j of b. Runs in O(rowsA * colsA * colsB) time and assumes the inner dimensions agree; it does not validate dimension compatibility and crashes on empty inputs because a[0] and b[0] are dereferenced unconditionally.
