A decorator factory that enforces a sliding-window rate limit of at most calls invocations per period seconds, tracking call timestamps in a deque using time.monotonic(). On each call it pops timestamps older than the cutoff window; if the remaining count is at or above calls it either raises RuntimeError with an estimated wait time (when raise_on_limit is True) or returns the configured default value, otherwise it appends the current timestamp and invokes the wrapped function. Validates calls > 0 and period > 0 at decoration time and exposes wrapper.reset() and wrapper.remaining() helpers; the implementation is not thread-safe.
