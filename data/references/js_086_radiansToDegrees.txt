Converts an angle given in radians to degrees using the inverse formula degrees = radians * 180 / Math.PI, in O(1) time and space. It returns a floating-point number; passing Math.PI yields 180 within IEEE 754 precision. The function does not validate input, so non-numeric arguments are coerced per JavaScript rules and produce NaN when coercion fails, while Infinity propagates as +/- Infinity. Negative radians produce negative degrees, and inputs outside the principal range are not normalized.
