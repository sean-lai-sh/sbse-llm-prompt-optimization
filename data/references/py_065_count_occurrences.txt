Recursively counts how many times target appears anywhere within an arbitrarily nested list/tuple structure, comparing against target with == at every node and leaf. Returns 1 immediately when nested itself equals target (which short-circuits descent into matching sub-structures), otherwise sums occurrences across child elements when nested is a list or tuple, and returns 0 for non-matching leaves. Runs in O(n) time relative to total nodes; note that a sub-list equal to target is counted as one occurrence and not traversed further.
