Computes the standard matrix product C = A * B for two 2D arrays and returns the resulting rowsA-by-colsB matrix. It validates that the inner dimensions match (colsA === B.length) and throws "Incompatible matrix dimensions" otherwise, then allocates a zero-filled result and accumulates dot products via the classic triple-nested i/j/k loop. Time complexity is O(rowsA * colsB * colsA) with O(rowsA * colsB) auxiliary space for the output. The function assumes both inputs are non-empty rectangular arrays of numbers and will throw on empty A or B due to the colsA / colsB lookups.
