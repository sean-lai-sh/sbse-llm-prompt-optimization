Finds the shortest contiguous substring of s that contains every character of t with at least the required multiplicity, using the standard expanding/contracting sliding window with two character-frequency Maps. The right pointer extends the window and increments a have counter when a character's window count exactly matches its needed count, then while have equals the number of distinct required characters the left pointer contracts to record any new minimum and decrement have when a needed character drops below its required count. Runs in O(|s| + |t|) time and returns "" when t is empty or no valid window exists.
