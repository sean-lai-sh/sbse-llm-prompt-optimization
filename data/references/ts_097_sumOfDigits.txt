Computes the sum of the decimal digits of an integer n by taking Math.abs(n), converting it to a string, splitting into characters, and reducing with Number coercion. Runs in O(d) time and space for d digits. Because conversion is purely string-based, a non-integer input contributes a NaN from the decimal point character (Number(".") is NaN) and the result becomes NaN; very large numbers serialized in exponential notation also produce NaN from the 'e' or '+' characters.
