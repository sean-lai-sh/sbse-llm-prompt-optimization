Computes n factorial iteratively by multiplying an accumulator initialized to 1 by each integer from 2 up to n inclusive. Runs in O(n) time and O(1) space using a simple for loop rather than recursion, avoiding stack growth. Returns 1 for n equal to 0 or 1 since the loop body does not execute, but does not handle negative inputs and silently overflows to Infinity for n greater than about 170 due to IEEE 754 double-precision limits.
