Groups objects in an array into buckets keyed by the string-coerced value of a specified property. It is generic over T, taking a T[] and a key of T and returning a Record<string, T[]> in O(n) time and O(n) space, preserving the original order of items within each bucket. The grouping key is forced through String() so non-string property values such as numbers or booleans collide with their string representations, and items are appended by reference rather than cloned.
