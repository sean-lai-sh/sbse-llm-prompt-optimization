Determines whether a string is a palindrome after normalization, returning True or False. It builds a cleaned string containing only alphanumeric characters lowercased and then compares it to its reversed slice. The check is O(n) in time and space, ignores punctuation, whitespace, and case (so phrases like "A man, a plan, a canal: Panama" are recognized), and treats an empty cleaned string as a palindrome by returning True.
