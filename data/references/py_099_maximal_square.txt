Finds the area of the largest all-ones square submatrix in a 2D grid of '0'/'1' (or 0/1) cells using a DP table where dp[i][j] holds the side length of the largest square whose bottom-right corner is at (i, j), computed as 1 + min(dp[i-1][j], dp[i][j-1], dp[i-1][j-1]) when the current cell is 1. Returns a tuple (area, position) where position is the (row, col) of the top-left corner of one such maximal square when return_position is True, otherwise None. Validates that all rows have equal length and that every cell is in {'0','1',0,1} (raising ValueError otherwise); runs in O(rows * cols) time and space.
