Determines whether a Gregorian year is a leap year using the standard rule: divisible by 4 and not by 100, unless also divisible by 400. The check is two short-circuited modulo operations and runs in O(1) time. The function does not validate that year is a positive integer and applies the proleptic Gregorian rule uniformly, so historical Julian-calendar years before 1582 are not handled specially.
