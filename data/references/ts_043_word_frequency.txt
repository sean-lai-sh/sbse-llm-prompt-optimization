Builds a Map from each word to its occurrence count after lowercasing the input, stripping every character that is not alphanumeric or whitespace, and splitting on whitespace runs. It accepts a string and returns a Map<string, number> in O(n) time and O(k) space, where k is the number of distinct words. Empty tokens produced by the split are filtered out, and the nullish coalescing operator ensures missing keys start at zero so the increment is always safe.
