Wraps fn in a memoizing closure that caches results in a Map keyed by JSON.stringify of the argument list, returning the cached value on subsequent calls with equivalent arguments. Lookup and insertion are amortized O(1) in the number of cached entries but O(k) in the serialized argument size, and the cache grows unboundedly for the lifetime of the returned function. Because keys are produced via JSON.stringify, arguments containing functions, undefined, symbols, circular references, or objects with differing key order may collide or throw, and this is preserved through fn.apply.
