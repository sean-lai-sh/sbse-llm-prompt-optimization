Recursively produces a deep copy of value, returning primitives and null directly, cloning Date and RegExp via their constructors, mapping arrays element-wise through deepClone, and otherwise creating a new object with the same prototype and recursively cloning each own enumerable string-keyed property. Runs in O(n) time and space proportional to the total node count of the input. Does not handle cyclic references and will recurse infinitely on them, ignores Symbol-keyed and non-enumerable properties, and does not specially clone Map, Set, TypedArray, or other built-in containers.
