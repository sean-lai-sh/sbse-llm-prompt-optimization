Recursively computes base raised to integer exp using exponentiation by squaring, returning a float. Negative exponents are handled by recursing on -exp and inverting the result, even exponents square the half-power result, and odd exponents recurse on exp-1 with one extra base multiplication. The algorithm runs in O(log exp) time and recursion depth; it returns 1.0 for exp == 0 (including base == 0 which is mathematically indeterminate) and does not guard against integer overflow or non-integer exponents.
