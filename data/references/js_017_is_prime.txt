Tests whether the integer n is prime by trial division with all candidates from 2 up to and including the floor of the square root of n. Returns false immediately for n less than 2 and otherwise returns true only if no divisor evenly divides n. Runs in O(sqrt(n)) time and O(1) space, but does not validate that n is a positive integer and may misbehave for non-integer or floating-point inputs.
