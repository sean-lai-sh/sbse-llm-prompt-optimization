Searches a sorted numeric array for a target by interpolating an estimated probe position from the linear distribution of values between arr[lo] and arr[hi]. It takes a number[] and a target number and returns the matching index or -1, running in O(log log n) time on uniformly distributed data and O(n) in the worst case, with O(1) space. The single-element subrange is handled explicitly to avoid division by zero, and the loop also exits when the target falls outside the current value range.
