Races a given promise against a timer to enforce an upper bound on its resolution time. It takes a promise and a millisecond budget ms, returning a new promise that resolves or rejects with the original promise if it settles first, or rejects with an Error reading "Timed out after {ms}ms" otherwise. The implementation uses Promise.race with a setTimeout-backed rejection. Note that the underlying promise is not actually cancelled when the timeout wins, and the setTimeout handle is not cleared, so its callback may still fire after the original promise settles.
