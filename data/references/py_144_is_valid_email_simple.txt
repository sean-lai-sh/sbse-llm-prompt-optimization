Performs a minimal structural validation of an email address by ensuring there is exactly one '@', that both the local part and domain are non-empty, and that the domain contains at least one '.' with no empty dot-separated segments. Returns False as soon as any check fails and True if all pass, running in O(n) time over the string due to count, split, and the any-check. The validation is intentionally simplistic and does not enforce RFC 5322 rules such as quoted locals, allowed characters, or top-level domain length.
