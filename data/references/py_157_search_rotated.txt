Performs a modified binary search to locate target in an integer array that was sorted ascending and then rotated at an unknown pivot, returning its index or -1 if absent. At each step it compares nums[lo] and nums[mid] to determine which half is sorted, then checks whether target falls in that sorted half to decide which side to discard. It runs in O(log n) time and O(1) space, correctly handles a non-rotated array as a degenerate case, and assumes all elements are distinct since duplicates can break the sortedness check.
