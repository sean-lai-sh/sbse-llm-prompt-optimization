Sorts arr in ascending order using a recursive three-way quicksort that picks the middle element as the pivot and partitions the array into less-than, equal-to, and greater-than buckets before recursively sorting the outer two and concatenating the results. Returns a new array and does not mutate the input. Average time is O(n log n) with O(n^2) worst case on adversarial inputs, and O(n) extra space per recursive level plus stack depth proportional to log n on average.
