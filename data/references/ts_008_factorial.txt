Computes n! recursively by multiplying n by factorial(n - 1) until the base case of n <= 1 returns 1. It accepts a number and returns a number, running in O(n) time with O(n) call-stack space, and throws a RangeError for negative inputs. It does not guard against non-integer arguments and overflows silently to Infinity beyond roughly n = 170 due to IEEE-754 double precision limits.
