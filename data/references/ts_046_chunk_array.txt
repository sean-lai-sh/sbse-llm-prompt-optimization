Splits an array into a list of contiguous subarrays of at most size elements each by stepping through the array in size-sized strides and slicing. It is generic over T, taking a T[] and a positive size and returning T[][] in O(n) time and O(n) space. A non-positive size triggers a RangeError, and the final chunk may be shorter than size when the array length is not an exact multiple; the original array is not mutated.
