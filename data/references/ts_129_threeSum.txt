Returns all unique triplets in nums that sum to zero by sorting a copy of the array ascending and then, for each anchor index i, using a two-pointer sweep with left = i+1 and right = n-1 to find pairs that complete the sum. Duplicate triplets are avoided by skipping repeated anchor values and by advancing both pointers past consecutive equal elements after a hit. Runs in O(n^2) time and O(n) extra space for the sorted copy; the input is not mutated and the empty result is returned when fewer than three elements exist.
