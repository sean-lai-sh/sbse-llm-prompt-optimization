Computes part as a percentage of total by returning (part / total) * 100, in O(1) time and space. It guards against division by zero by returning 0 when total === 0, avoiding Infinity or NaN for that case. The function returns a floating-point number and supports negative values (which produce negative percentages) and values where part exceeds total (yielding percentages above 100). It does not validate the inputs are numeric, so non-numeric arguments follow JavaScript's arithmetic coercion and may produce NaN.
