Converts the input string str to title case by lowercasing it, splitting on single space characters, uppercasing the first character of each token, and joining the tokens back with single spaces. Runs in O(n) time and O(n) space and does not mutate the input. Only splits on the literal space character, so tabs, newlines, and runs of multiple spaces are not handled as word separators, and non-Latin alphabets are subject to JavaScript's default case mappings.
